About

Greensprings School opened its Lekki Campus in 2005, this was specifically built as a fit-for-school purpose to meet the ever-changing needs of 21st century education. The campus has over 1400 pupils from pre-school age through to IB Diploma students (2 yrs to 18 yrs).
